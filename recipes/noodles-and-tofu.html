Description

This Noodles & Tofu dish is the perfect weekday healthy meal to make in a rush!

Using a lot of different spices ensures that the normally bland tofu gets a very tasty flavour.

Ingredients (4 persons)

  • Noodles - 400 grams
  • Tofu - 500 grams

Steps

  1. Dry the tofu in a towel
  2. Cook the noodles
